## Nvoids.com Structure

From initial scan: Jobs Nvoids is a **free job posting board** used by US staffing firms. It features:
- Free job posting (always free)
- Telegram group integration (@ustaffing)
- Email subscription for job alerts
- WhatsApp integration for hotlists
- IPv6 support for fast access
- Mix of IT staffing requirements (Oracle, AWS, AEM, Workday, AI/ML)

## Key Insight
The primary pain point is **fragmentation** — recruiters check 5+ channels daily (LinkedIn, Dice, WhatsApp, Telegram, email). Hotlists come in unstructured formats. No single source of truth exists for available consultants or open requirements.

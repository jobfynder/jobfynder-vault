# 05 — Offer

Assets → Workflows → Activation.

**Question:** What do users get and why should they pay?

**Product Line:** Connect. Submit. Place.

- **Connect** — Build verified relationships across recruiters, BSRs, consultants, employers, and service providers.
- **Submit** — Turn requirements, hotlists, resumes into structured, trackable submissions.
- **Place** — Improve visibility, trust, and follow-through until outcome is known.

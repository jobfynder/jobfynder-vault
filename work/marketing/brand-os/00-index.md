## The 12 Pillars

**Part 1 — Brand Method: Who Jobfynder Is**
01. DNA — Purpose, Vision, Mission, Values
02. Positioning — Market, Opportunity, Position
03. Narrative — Messaging, Story, Naming, Hooks
04. Identity — Personality, Verbal, Visual

**Part 2 — Brand Mode: What Jobfynder Does**
05. Offer — Assets, Workflows, Activation
06. Funnel — Acquisition, Conversion, Retention
07. Marketing — Creative, Campaigns, Distribution
08. Operations — Execution, Systems, Evolution

**Part 3 — Brand Mind: How Jobfynder Decides**
09. Discover — Signal gathering
10. Diagnose — Problem analysis
11. Decide — Build or not build
12. Deploy — Measure impact

## Guardrails

**Always feel like:** trusted staffing control room, relationship intelligence layer, verified professional network.

**Never feel like:** job board, ATS clone, social network, spam tool.

## Ship Checklist

Before anything ships — does it:
1. Build trust?
2. Preserve context?
3. Increase transparency?
4. Strengthen a staffing relationship?
5. Move users closer to a real placement?

No to 3+ → don't ship.

## Key Takeaways for Jobfynder

Most top recruiting blogs share common traits:
1. **Data-driven content** — original research, surveys, and statistics
2. **Practical how-to guides** — actionable advice recruiters can use immediately
3. **Industry news analysis** — timely commentary on market shifts
4. **Tool comparisons** — honest reviews and comparisons of recruiting tech
5. **Community voices** — guest posts from practitioners, not just marketing teams
